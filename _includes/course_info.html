Textbook

There is no required textbook. I recommend either Fundamentals of Computer Graphics by P. Shirley or Foundations of 3D Computer Graphics by S. Gortler. For a reference for modern graphics programming: OpenGL ES 3.0 Programming Guide by D. Ginsburg, et. al. There are also numerous helpful tutorial sites for example: http://learnopengl.com/
